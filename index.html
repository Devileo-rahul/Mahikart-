The Journey of Us 🎀

From a stranger to someone special — every step of the way

#MAHI-2805
K

1. First Glance — Pehli Nazar

Tu class aur university ki pehli ladki thi jise maine notice kiya tha. Par tujhse baat karne ki zero himmat thi mere andar. Das aaste-jaate "Hi" aur "Hello" hota tha, aur teri ek nazar pe main pagal ho jaata tha. 😅

R

2. Connection Failed — Flop Masterplans

Tere kareeb aane ke liye maine bohot jugaad lagaye. Apne dost ka sahara liya, tere doston ko car mein invite kiya... par sab flop! Main haar maan ke life mein busy ho gaya. Luckily "Fuddu Log" waale group mein hum saath the.

G

3. Dispatched — March Magic

March mein aakhir humari dosti ho hi gayi. Aur tab wo destiny wala moment aaya — jab mujhe pata chala ki Bhiwani ki ladki! Hisar mein mere ghar ke bilkul paas apni Tai ke yahan rehti hai! Kya coincidence tha yaar! 😅

B

4. In Transit — Baarish, Bike & Vada Pav

Hum jab bhi milte hain toofan ya baarish kyun aati hai? Wo pehli baarish wali bike ride bina kisi dost ke... aur Vada Pav ke liye humara common pyaar — wahin se bond strong hone laga. 🌧️❤️

💬

5. Internship Ke Din — Chat Pe Sach 💬

Internship ke waqt — humari baat chat pe hoti thi. Ek din maine apne puraane "flop masterplans" ki poori kahani tujhe chat pe bata di — woh sab kuch jo maine tujhse karne ki koshish ki thi. Tujhe sun ke shocked zaroor hua hoga 😂 — woh expression imagine kar ke aaj bhi hasi aati hai. Tab pehli baar laga ki sach bol diya. 😅

🚫

6. Signal Lost — The Block 😶

Phir meri galti ki wajah se... aur bas — Classic teri reaction — seedha block! 😅 Na koi explanation ka mauka, na koi sai. Poori screen pe sirf "Message Failed" wala feel tha. Kuch waqt ke liye sab khamoshi mein dub gaya.

Ek lafz ne dil tod diya tha mera,
Screen pe "Blocked" — aur main thoda sa toot gaya. 💔
Par andar se pata tha — ye kahani adhuri hai,
Kuch toh tha jo khatam nahi hua tha. 🌸
✅

7. Reconnected — 13 January Magic ✨

13 January is saal — finally hum aamne-saamne mile aur saari confusions clear ho gayi! Jo baat chat pe shuru hui thi, woh real life mein aaj seedhi ho gayi. Ek mulaqat mein jaise poori zindagi ka bojh utar gaya. 🥲

💛

8. Plot Twist — 150km & Tu Ne Wapas Joda 💛

13 Jan ke kuch din baad — meri zid ne mujhe 150 km door tere office tak pahuncha diya. Bina mile, khali haath wapas aa gaya. Us ek foolish trip ki wajah se humara poora group toot gaya. 💔 Par jab sab ek doosre se door ho gaye — tu thi jo sab ko wapas layi, sab ko phir se ikatha kiya. Jo group tootne ka reason ban gaya tha, usse tune hi wapas joda. 💛

150 km ka safar, seedha tere darwaze tak,
Himmat nahi hui — wapas aa gaya, khali haath. 😔
Par kuch rishtey toote nahi karte,
Woh dil mein hamesha rehte hain. 💛
"Group toot gaya, sab alag ho gaye —
Par tune wapas sab ko joda. 💛
Kuch log sirf dost nahi hote,
Tu ghar hoti hai. 🏡"
"Tune aakhir mujhe unblock kiya,
Chalo ganeemat hai bhai... 😅
Dekho aaj teri saari UPI payments karna,
Sab mere hi haath hai!" 😅❤️
